My Top 5 Favorite Telugu Movies

  1. Baahubali: The Beginning
    • Year Released: 2015
    • Main Actors: Prabhas, Rana Daggubati, Anushka Shetty, Tamannaah
    • Description: In ancient India, an adventurous and daring man becomes involved in a decades-old feud between two warring peoples.
  2. Arjun Reddy
    • Year Released: 2017
    • Main Actors: Vijay Deverakonda, Shalini Pandey
    • Description: A brilliant medical student with anger management issues goes down a self-destructive path after the love of his life is forced to marry another man.
  3. Magadheera
    • Year Released: 2009
    • Main Actors: Ram Charan, Kajal Aggarwal, Dev Gill, Srihari
    • Description: A brave warrior of the past reincarnates into the present to save his love from a dangerous villain.
  4. Ala Vaikunthapurramuloo
    • Year Released: 2020
    • Main Actors: Allu Arjun, Pooja Hegde, Tabu, Jayaram
    • Description: Bantu grows up being constantly subjected to his father's scorn. After learning about his biological parents, he decides to carve a place for himself within the family he truly belongs to.
  5. Mahanati
    • Year Released: 2018
    • Main Actors: Keerthy Suresh, Dulquer Salmaan, Samantha Akkineni, Vijay Deverakonda
    • Description: A biographical film about the life of legendary South Indian actress Savitri.
